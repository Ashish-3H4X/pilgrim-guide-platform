# Product Requirements

## Problem

Travelers visiting pilgrimage cities often struggle to find reliable information about temples, stay options, food places, and nearby attractions.

Most platforms focus only on hotels or reviews but do not provide structured guidance for religious travelers.

## Solution

Pilgrim Guide provides a simple platform where users can select a city and instantly view important travel information.

## Main Features

1. Temple Information
- Opening time
- Closing time
- Temple rules

2. Famous Places
- Popular attractions
- Location information

3. Stay Options
- Dharamshala
- Budget hotels

4. Food Information
- Bhandara locations
- Prasad halls
- Affordable restaurants

5. Route Suggestions
- Recommended order of visiting places

6. Map Navigation
- Directions using maps

## Target Users

- Pilgrimage travelers
- Budget travelers
- Foreign tourists visiting temples
